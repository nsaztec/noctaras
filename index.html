How it works

Three steps to understanding

Noctaras makes the complex world of dream psychology accessible.

1

Describe your dream

Write what you remember — the places, the people, the feelings. Do not worry about structure, simply pour out the fragmented narrative exactly as you experienced it.

2

AI reads the symbols

Noctaras draws from Carl Jung's archetypes, ancient cultural symbolism, and deep psychological theories to uncover the hidden narrative beneath the surface.

3

Save to your journal

Every interpretation is encrypted and stored in your private journal. Watch patterns and repeating symbols emerge, learning the language your subconscious speaks in.
